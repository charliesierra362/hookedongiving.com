About the Event

A Day on the River.
A Night to Remember.

Join us for an unforgettable day of philanthropy on Wyoming's legendary North Platte River. Cast your line alongside experienced local guides during the tournament, then join us for an elegant evening gala featuring dinner, live music, a live auction, and exciting raffles.

Every bid, every ticket, every donation goes directly to empowering victims and inspiring hope across Converse and Niobrara Counties through the work of the Hope Center.

Secure your spot on the legendary North Platte River through competitive bidding. The top 20 bids win access to this exclusive tournament — with each team paired with an experienced local guide.

Submit Your Bid

Minimum bid is $5,000 per boat (2 participants). Whole dollars only. Download the bid form and submit to aneubauer@caminoresources.com by March 27, 2026 at 5:00 PM MST.

02
🏆
Winners Selected

The top 20 highest bids secure a boat spot. In the event of a tie, remaining spots are resolved by random draw. Winners notified by March 30, 2026.

03
💳
Complete Payment

Full payment is due by April 10, 2026. Unpaid spots are forfeited to the next highest bidder. All bids are non-refundable tax-deductible donations.
